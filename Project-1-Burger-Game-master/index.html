How to Play

Ingredients will fall from the four pipes at the top of the screen.

The customer's order is shown in the top right.

All you have to do is catch the ingredients in the correct order by moving your bun with the Left and Right arrow keys!

Be careful, though -- the better you do, the harder your job will get, and one mistake will cost you everything!
